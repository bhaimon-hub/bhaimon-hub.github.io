About Broxy Hub

Welcome to **Broxy Hub**, a premier digital destination for the global gaming community. Managed and developed by **Broxy**, our mission is to empower players through data-driven tools and interactive experiences.

Originally starting as a project to assist players in **Mobile Legends: Bang Bang**, Broxy Hub has evolved into a multi-functional platform featuring a comprehensive **Hero Counter Database**, a high-engagement **Lucky Spin** simulator, and a curated archive of high-quality gaming media.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of the utmost importance at Broxy Hub. We use industry-standard practices to ensure your data remains secure.

  • Cookies: We use cookies to analyze site traffic and personalize your experience. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits.
  • Data Collection: We do not collect or sell personal identification information unless explicitly provided via contact forms.
  • API Usage: This site utilizes the **YouTube Data API v3** to provide video content. By using this site, you are also bound by the Google Privacy Policy.
